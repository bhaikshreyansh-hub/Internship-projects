Company Leave Policy

All full-time employees are entitled to 18 days of paid annual leave per calendar year.
Leave accrues monthly at a rate of 1.5 days per month and unused leave can be carried
forward up to a maximum of 10 days into the next calendar year.

Sick leave is provided separately, with employees entitled to 10 days of paid sick leave
per year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 3 consecutive days.

Employees must submit leave requests through the HR portal at least 5 working days in
advance for planned leave. Emergency leave can be approved retroactively by a direct
manager, but must be logged within 48 hours of return.

Maternity leave is granted for 26 weeks as per statutory requirements, and paternity
leave is granted for 2 weeks. Both can be extended unpaid with manager approval.

Public holidays are observed as per the regional calendar published by HR each January
and do not count against an employee's annual leave balance.
