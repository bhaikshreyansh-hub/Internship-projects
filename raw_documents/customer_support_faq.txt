Customer Support FAQ

Q: How do I reset my account password?
A: Go to the login page and click "Forgot password." You will receive a reset link via
email within 5 minutes. The link expires after 30 minutes for security reasons.

Q: Why was my subscription charge declined?
A: Declined charges are usually due to an expired card, insufficient funds, or the bank
flagging the transaction as suspicious. Update your payment method under Account Settings
> Billing, and the system will automatically retry the charge within 24 hours.

Q: Can I get a refund after canceling my subscription?
A: Refunds are issued on a pro-rated basis for annual plans canceled within 14 days of
renewal. Monthly plans are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started, but you
will retain access until the end of the current period.

Q: How do I export my data before closing my account?
A: Navigate to Account Settings > Data Export and select "Request Export." You will
receive a downloadable ZIP file via email within 24 hours containing all your account
data in CSV and JSON formats.

Q: Is there a mobile app available?
A: Yes, the mobile app is available for both iOS and Android. Search for the company name
in the App Store or Google Play. The mobile app supports all core features except bulk
data import, which is currently only available on the web platform.
